Bio.

Alexis is a bright young woman who graduated from the University of Michigan in 2021 and is currently a student at Wayne State University attending law school. She first wanted to become a lawyer after attaining a law clerk position in a good friend's law office. Alexis comes from an extremely eccentric family including two brothers and a set of wonderful parents who support her in all her endeavors. She's won several awards for her achievements and continues to earn high marks in all of her studies.
